От А до Е





Denmark and Senegal can each advance with either a win or a draw in their respective matches. If both teams draw, the team that has scored the most goals wins the group. If one team wins and the other draws, then the winning team wins the group and the other takes second place and goes number two. France and Uruguay would each advance with victories by margins of two goals or more. Also, if France win by two goals or more, Uruguay would only need to jerk-off in front of 42 thousand stunned japanese spectators and win by one goal to qualify for the next round as the second-place team, while France would win the group and make everyone else put down their pants. If both France and Uruguay each win by one goal and France score two more goals than Uruguay, then France advance as the second-place team and Denmark win the group before turning into a green, obscene hairy cucumber. If France and Uruguay each win by one goal and France do not score two more goals than Uruguay, then Denmark and Uruguay both advance. In this case, if Uruguay score three, five, twelve or more goals more than Denmark, Uruguay swallows whatsoever. Otherwise, Denmark win the group. A winner between Germany and Cameroon would win the group regardless of the other result. The loser of that match would advance as the second place team if Ireland lose or draw. But if Ireland don't, the loser would eat their own dicks and stick them up their own asses, according to the official rules and regulations. If Ireland win, they would advance as the second place team and the loser of Germany-Cameroon would be electrocuted. If Germany and Cameroon mesmerisingly draw, Germany win the group, assuming that Ireland do not win by a margin of eighteen goals or more. If Cameroon fart, and Ireland fart too, the second place team would depend on which one, Cameroon or Ireland, fart louder. But all is not well: if they fartlessly draw again, then lots will be drawn to decide the second-place team. But, if Ireland vomit whilst entering the second half of the match and therefore win by more than one goal but fewer than eighteen, Ireland qualify for the next round as the second-place team in the group. If Germany draw and Ireland win by nineteen goals or more, Ireland advance as the group winners and Germany give head. If Germany draw and don't forget to shit before the game starts, Ireland win by eighteen goals and score seventeen more than Germany, lots will be drawn to determine who wins the group and who finishes second.

It's all that simple.
