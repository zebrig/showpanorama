Once upon a time



Once upon a time there was Zajka, indeed he was the most mischievous of all animals. One day he was in a very good humor, for he had made a ball of shit with the power of turning all that was good and beautiful when it was thrown, to look poor and mean.

And it was to those woods that Zajka came, holding up the ball in his hand.

By and by Medvezhonok wandered near the lane, and looking up, saw little Zajka lurking in the shades of a pussy willow.



«Isn't that glorious fun!» Zajka said, throwing the ball onto Medvezhonok.

«Aw shit!» yelled Medvezhonok as the ball hit him.

So Zajka went joyously home, and no one ever did anything to harm him again.
